INDIAN COUNCIL OF MEDICAL RESEARCH

Serving the Nation Since 1911

The Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR), New Delhi, is the apex body in India for the formulation, coordination and promotion of biomedical research. Established in 1911, it is one of the oldest medical research bodies in the world.

Jodhpur
ICMR National Institute of Health Research (NIHR), Jodhpur
(ICMR-NIHR)

Jodhpur, Estd. 1984

The National Institute for Health Research (NIHR), Jodhpur, was established on 27 June 1984 as the Desert Medicine Research Centre (DMRC). It was later renamed the National Institute for Implementation Research on Non-Communicable Diseases (NIIRNCD) on 7 December 2019 and subsequently redesignated as NIHR on 29 April 2026.

NIHR focuses on generating high-impact research across priority domains, strengthening capacity through specialized training programmes, and developing effective behaviour change communication strategies to address non-communicable disease (NCD) risk factors.
