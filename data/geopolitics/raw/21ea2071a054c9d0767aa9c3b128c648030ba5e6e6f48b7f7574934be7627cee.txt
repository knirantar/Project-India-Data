Overview


At the end of April, overall conditions are positive for wheat, maize, rice, and soybeans. In the Northern Hemisphere, conditions are generally favourable; however, rainfall is needed in parts of Europe and in the US. For maize, harvesting is ongoing in the Southern Hemisphere, and sowing is expanding in the Northern Hemisphere. Rice conditions are mostly favourable as harvests progress in South and Southeast Asia and South America. For soybeans, harvesting is progressing with good yields in the Southern Hemisphere as sowing ramps up in the Northern Hemisphere.

Australia

United States
