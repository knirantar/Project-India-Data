CSK IPL 2026 Playoffs Qualification Scenario: 
Despite labouring through a difficult start, losing three matches on the trot at the start of their campaign, Chennai Super Kings fought back hard to keep their IPL 2026 Playoffs hopes alive. However, the five-time champions were dealt a blow when they lost to Lucknow Super Giants by seven wickets.

Lucknow
, 
CSK
 had the chance to take a crucial step ahead towards the Playoffs while also making the top-4 race all the more intense had they trounced the Super Giants for the second time in five days. CSK would have gone into the match on Friday with a lot of promise due to the fact that they broke an eight-year drought when they chased down a 204-run target against Lucknow at Chepauk, a feat they had not managed since 2018.


CSK would’ve become the fourth team to reach 14 points had they beaten 
LSG
, even pipping the 
Punjab Kings
, who had started the season unbeaten across seven matches, before dropping five matches on the bounce and standing currently on 13 points.


CSK IPL 2026 Playoffs Qualification scenario


After the defeat to Lucknow, CSK are slated to take on the 
Gujarat Titans
 and the 
Sunrisers Hyderabad
 in their remaining matches, both of whom are placed in the second and third spots currently.




If CSK can win both of their remaining games, they will finish on 16 points wherein the Net Run Rate could come into the play to decide their finish in the standings, most likely whittling down to an Eliminator spot with a third or fourth-placed finish.


If CSK only win one of their three remaining matches, then the Super Kings are all but out of the Playoffs race as both 
PBKS
 and 
Rajasthan Royals
 can breach the 14-point mark.
