KKR vs GT IPL 2026 Highlights: 
Gujarat Titans never really looked capable of posing a serious challenge to the target of 248 set by Kolkata Knight Riders for them despite having Shubman Gill, Jos Buttler and Sai Sudharsan in the middle for a majority of the chase. Sudharsan was forced to retire hurt after getting off to a good start but came back after Gill fell towards the latter stages of the innings. He finished unbeaten on 53 off 28 while Gill fell on 84 off 49. Jos Buttler fell on 57 off 29. Sunil Narine was the star once again for KKR, finishing with figures of 2/29 while Saurabh Dubey and Cameron Green got a wicket each.
