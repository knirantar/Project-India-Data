    PBKS vs RCB Highlights, IPL 2026: RCB defeated PBKS on Sunday.

    PBKS vs RCB Highlights, IPL 2026: Defending champions Royal Challengers Bengaluru became the first team to qualify for the IPL 2026 playoffs, defeating Punjab Kings by 23 runs. Chasing 223 runs, PBKS were restricted to 199/8 in 20 overs. Rasikh Salam Dar took a three-wicket haul, removing Shashank SIngh (56) and Azmatullah Omarzai (37) in the final over. Shashank began a late fight back with Marcus Stoinis, but the latter lost his wicket to Josh Hazlewood, departing for 37 off 25 balls. PBKS had an early batting collapse and their last top order batter to depart for the dugout was Suryansh Shedge (35), who lost his wicket to Suyash Sharma. Rasikh Salam Dar struck in the fourth over, removing PBKS captain Shreyas Iyer (1). Meanwhile, Bhuvneshwar Kumar dismissed Priyansh Arya (0) and Prabhsimran Singh (5). Royal Challengers Bengaluru posted 222/4 in 20 overs, setting a target of 223 runs. Venkatesh Iyer remained unbeaten at 73* off 40 balls.

    Meanwhile, Arshdeep Singh removed Tim David (28) in the final ball. Yuzvendra Chahal struck in the final ball of the 15th over, removing Virat Kohli. The RCB opener failed to find the fence, departing for 58 off 37 balls. Harpreet Brar struck again in the 10th over, removing Devdutt Padikkal (45). Earlier, Royal Challengers Bengaluru lost Jacob Bethell as the opener fell to Harpreet Brar in the third over. Initially, Punjab Kings captain Shreyas Iyer won the toss and opted to bowl against Royal Challengers Bengaluru. RCB were without regular captain Rajat Patidar and Jitesh Sharma was named as the stand-in skipper.
