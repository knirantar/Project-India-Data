Who won CSK vs LSG IPL 2026 match yesterday?


Chasing 204 for win against LSG, Chennai pulled off their first 200-plus chase since the 2018 final, thanks to a breathtaking assault from Urvil Patel. The young batter smashed the joint-fastest fifty in IPL history, reaching the milestone in just 13 balls en route to a 23-ball 65 studded with eight sixes. After opener Sanju Samson fell for 28, Urvil stitched an 81-run stand off 34 deliveries with skipper Ruturaj Gaikwad (42). CSK lost their way mid-innings, slipping from 126/2 to needing 10 off the final over. 
Shivam Dube
 held his nerve, smashing two sixes to seal the win with a ball to spare.


Earlier, Josh Inglis’ explosive 85 off 33 balls powered LSG to 203/8. But Jamie Overton’s three wickets triggered a middle-order collapse before Shahbaz Ahmed’s 43* lifted them past 200. With the win, CSK remain alive in the playoff race and are placed fifth in the standing with 12 points.
